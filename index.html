Welcome to Civin Design Institute

CIVIN DESIGN
INSTITUTE

A place for all design knowledge, practical learning and refining skills to help achieve the dreams of budding designers and engineers.

To create professionals that believe in good quality work and understand the importance of value-based learning.

To place students in major companies and encourage them to think global and act local.

Creating and helping the youth to become the best version of themselves in their chosen field.

At Civin Design Institute, we're more than just a training institute – we're a hub of creativity, passion, and innovation dedicated to shaping the future of design and engineering in Ahilyanagar. Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, we empower aspiring designers and engineers with the skills and confidence to excel in this dynamic industry.

Our team comprises experienced professionals and industry experts who are deeply committed to providing hands-on training and personalized guidance. We believe in fostering a learning environment where creativity flourishes and students gain a comprehensive understanding of design principles, software proficiency, and practical execution.

Whether you're looking to embark on a new career path or enhance your existing skills, Civil Design Institute offers a range of meticulously crafted courses designed to meet your individual goals. Join us and become part of a vibrant community that's passionate about creating inspiring designs.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What courses do you offer?

We offer comprehensive training in Civil Engineering, Interior Design, and Architecture software including SketchUp, Revit (Architecture & Structure), AutoCAD, Staad Pro, Estimation & Costing, Enscape, Lumion, D5 Render, 3ds Max, and Navisworks.

Do you offer online classes?

Yes! We offer both Online and Offline training options. Students can choose based on their convenience and location. Live interactive sessions with recorded backups.

What are the class timings?

We have flexible batches: Morning batch from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM and Evening batch from 4:00 PM to 8:00 PM. Weekend batches also available.

Do you provide placement assistance?

Yes, we provide 100% placement assistance to our students upon course completion. We have a dedicated placement cell that conducts mock interviews, resume workshops, and campus drives.

Is your institute certified?

Yes, we are an ISO Certified Company, maintaining the highest standards of education and training excellence. Our diploma courses are government approved.

Do you provide lifetime access to study materials?

Yes, students receive lifetime access to all lecture recordings, free software installation, and ongoing technical support even after course completion.

What is the duration of the course?

Course durations vary from 3 to 6 months depending on the specialization. We also offer fast-track and customised courses as per student requirements.

Do you offer demo classes?

Yes, we offer free demo classes for all our courses. You can attend a demo session before enrolling to understand our teaching methodology.
